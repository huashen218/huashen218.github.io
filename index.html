I am a tenure-track assistant professor of Computer Science at NYU Shanghai, affiliated with the Computer Science Department at New York University.
I broadly pursue interdisciplinary research connecting AI/ML with HCI/Social Computing. Specifically, I initiated the research on Bidirectional Human-AI Alignment (NeurIPS 2025 Position Paper, ICLR & CHI 2025 Workshop, NeurIPS 2025 Tutorial, NYU Shanghai Course). My research aims to:

"Maximizing Capabilities AND Minimizing Risks in Human-AI Co-Evolution"


My study enables humans to interactively explain, audit, and collaborate with agentic AI (Align Humans with AI), while integrates human feedback and values to improve agentic AI (Align AI with Humans), achieving a complete loop of Bidirectional Humans↔AI Alignment.
I will be recruiting PhD students through NYU Courant Computer Science Department starting in 2026 Fall. I'm also recruiting Undergraduate and Master students in 2025. See our BiAlign Lab page.
About Me
I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Washington iSchool and RAISE Center (2024-2025), and a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University of Michigan UMSI and CSE (2023-2024).
