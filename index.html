RESEARCH INTEREST

I am interested in general area of AI and HCI. My current research focuses on improving human-centric deep learning interpretability for natural language processing and computer vision applications, such as generating visual and text explanation for deep learning and evaluating AI explanation from human perspective. Revolving the goal, my current topics include:

  • Explainable AI (XAI), Human-AI Interaction, AI Model Robustness.
  • Dialogue Systems, Question Answering, Natural Language Reasoning, Knowledge Graph.
