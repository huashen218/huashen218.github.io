SHORT BIO

Hi there🤗! I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at PennState University working on Explainable AI within the NLP + HCI areas. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Ting-hao (Kenneth) Huang at the terrific CrowdAI Lab. I'm also closely working with Dr. Sherry Tongshuang Wu from CMU fortunately. Additionally, I appreciate the wonderful year working with Dr. Brian D. Davison at Lehigh University. Before this, I've received my M.S. and B.S. degrees from the Renmin University of China and University of Science and Technology Beijing, respectively.

RESEARCH INTEREST

I am broadly interested in the research about human-centered AI explanation and fairness, human-AI alignment and interaction, AI-assisted learning support systems, conversational AI, etc.

Also, I've conducted much speech processing research during my research interns with the fantastic Google AI Research and Amazon Alexa AI teams. My amazing advisors include Dr. Andreas Stolcke, Dr. Vicky Zayats, Dr. Yuguang Yang, Dr. Jari Kolehmainen, etc. I appreciate all of them here!
