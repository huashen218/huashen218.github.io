I am a tenure-track assistant professor of Computer Science at NYU Shanghai, jointly affiliated with the Computer Science Department at New York University. I serve on NYU Shanghai's AI & Innovation Advisory Committee, where I lead the Interdisciplinary AI Research subcommittee. I have been honored to receive four Shanghai talent awards: Pearl Elite, Pujiang, Magnolia, and Oriental.

I broadly pursue interdisciplinary research connecting AI (e.g., Natural Language Processing, Speech Processing, and Machine Learning) and Humans (e.g, Human-Computer Interaction, Computational Social Science, Psychology). Specifically, I initiated the research on Bidirectional Human-AI Alignment (NeurIPS 2025 Tutorial | CHI 2026 Workshop | NeurIPS 2025 Position Paper | ICLR & CHI 2025 Workshop | NYU Shanghai Course). My research aims to:

"Maximizing Capabilities AND Minimizing Risks in Human-AI Co-Evolution"

that enables humans to interactively explain, audit, and collaborate with agentic AI (Align Humans with AI), and integrates human feedback and values to improve agentic AI (Align AI with Humans), achieving a complete loop of Bidirectional Humans↔AI Alignment. Concrete research directions and representative papers include:
1. Minimizing Risks
  • Value Alignment: Mind the Value-Action Gap (EMNLP 2025 Outstanding Paper); Value Alignment Tax (ACL 2026 Findings); Deep Value Benchmark (NeurIPS 2025); ValueCompass (EMNLP 2025 WiNLP); ValueFlow (Arxiv 2026)
  • Agentic AI Safety & Risks: LLM Dark Patterns (CHI 2026 Best Paper Honorable Mention); SusBench (IUI 2026)
    • 2. Maximizing Capabilities
  • Epistemic & Cognitive Alignment: ConvXAI (CSCW 2023 Best Demo); Epistemic Alignment (COLM 2025); PromptAuditor (CHI 2025)
  • Behavior & Representation Alignment: HypoCompass (AIED 2024 / IJCAI 2025 Best Paper & Best Interactive Event); Bridging Human Interpretation and Machine Representation (CHI 2026 BiAlign)
    • 3. Dynamics & Co-evolution
  • Bidirectional Human-AI Alignment: Bidirectional Human-AI Alignment (NeurIPS 2025 Position); Human-AI Alignment Tutorial (NeurIPS 2025 Tutorial); Human-AI Interaction Alignment (CHI 2026); Bidirectional Opinion Dynamics (CHI 2026 BiAlign); Bidirectional Alignment (ICLR 2025)
