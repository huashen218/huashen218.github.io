SHORT BIO

Hi there🤗! I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at PennState University working on Explainable AI within the NLP + HCI areas. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Ting-hao (Kenneth) Huang at the terrific CrowdAI Lab. I'm also, fortunately, working closely with Dr. Sherry Tongshuang Wu at Carnegie Mellon University. Additionally, I appreciate the wonderful year working with Dr. Brian D. Davison at Lehigh University. Before this, I've received my M.S. and B.S. degrees from the Renmin University of China and University of Science and Technology Beijing, respectively.

RESEARCH INTEREST

I am broadly interested in the area of Human-AI Interaction. My current research interests lie in facilitating the usefulness of explainable AI algorithms from human-centered perspective. I'm particularly interested in improving human-centered AI interpretability and fairness in speech and natural language processing, conversational AI, and other deep learning fields.

Meanwhile, I am also working on speech processing research during my research internships with the fantastic Google AI Research Speech Intelligence Team, Amazon Alexa AI Speaker and ASR Language Modeling teams. My amazing advisors include Dr. Andreas Stolcke, Dr. Ariya Rastrow, Dr. Jasha Droppo, Dr. Jari Kolehmainen, Dr. Vicky Zayats, Dr. Yuguang Yang, etc. I appreciate all of them here!
