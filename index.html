I am a tenure-track assistant professor of Computer Science at NYU Shanghai, affiliated with the NYU Tandon School of Engineering and NYU Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences.
I broadly pursue interdisciplinary research connecting AI/ML with HCI/Social Computing. Specifically, I initiated the research on Bidirectional Human-AI Alignment (Systematic Review, ICLR & CHI 2025 Workshop, NeurIPS 2025 Tutorial, NYU Shanghai Course). My research aims to:

Maximize Co-performance and Minimize Harms in Human-AI Co-evolvement


My study enables humans to interactively explain, audit, and collaborate with agentic AI (Align Humans with AI), while integrates human feedback and values to improve agentic AI (Align AI with Humans), achieving a complete loop of Bidirectional Humans↔AI Alignment.
I will be recruiting PhD students through NYU Courant Institute and NYU Tandon School starting in 2026 Fall. I'm also recruiting Undergraduate and Master students in 2025. See our BiAlign Lab page.
About Me
I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Washington iSchool and RAISE Center (2024-2025), and a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University of Michigan UMSI and CSE (2023-2024).
