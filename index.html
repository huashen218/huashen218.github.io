SHORT BIO

Currently, I am a third-year Ph.D. student in IST College at PennState University, I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Ting-hao (Kenneth) Huang at amazing CrowdAI Lab. I received my M.S. in School of Information at the Renmin University of China, advised by Dr. Xun Liang. I received my B.S. in Information Security at the University of Science and Technology Beijing.

In 2021 summer, I am an applied scientist intern at Amazon Alexa AI working on fairness in speaker verification with amazing supervisors Dr. Yuguang Yang, Dr. Andreas Stolcke, Dr. Jasha Droppo, Dr. Christine Han, etc. I also appreciate the time working with Prof. Ting Wang at PennState and the wonderful year working with Prof. Brian D. Davison from Lehigh University.

RESEARCH INTEREST

I am broadly interested in the area of human interaction with AI machine. My current research interests lie in facilitating the usefulness of ethical AI techniques from human-centered perspective. I'm particularly interested in improving human-centered AI interpretability and fairness in speech and natural language processing, conversational AI, and other deep learning fields.
